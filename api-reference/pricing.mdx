## Rate Limits

StealthGPT API has the following rate limits to ensure optimal performance and fair usage:

- **3,500 RPM** (Requests Per Minute)
- **350,000 TPM** (Tokens Per Minute)

## How to Purchase

### Initial Token Purchase
1. Sign in to your account at [stealthgpt.ai/stealthapi](https://www.stealthgpt.ai/stealthapi)
2. Use the token slider to select your desired amount (between 1M and 10M tokens)
3. Complete the purchase through our secure payment system

### Additional Token Purchases
Once you have an account, you can purchase additional tokens through your dashboard at [stealthgpt.ai/stealthapi/dashboard/billing](https://www.stealthgpt.ai/stealthapi/dashboard/billing)

Your tokens will be immediately available for use after purchase.
